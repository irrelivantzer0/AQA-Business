Unit 1 - What is business?

Understanding the nature and purpose of a business

The purpose of a business is to:

  • Create employment
  • Create wealth
  • Create new products and services
  • To enhance a country's reputaition

Mission - the aims of a business or what they want to do in the future, their sole purpose.

Mission Statement - it's a qualitative statement of the busiensses aims where they use language to motivate employees and convince customers.

Business objectives - are designed to help the busienss to achieve their overall aims.

Corporate objectives - govern the objectives set by eachdepartment or division of a busienss.

Functional objectives - goals of HR, finance, marketing and operational departments.
